Introduction

NLP Academic Exchange Platform (NICE) is a channel where we regularly invite researchers and startup founders to talk about their research or working experience, mainly in the field of artificial intelligence, especially natural language processing and large language models. We are a non-profit organization consisting mainly of young researchers in academic.
